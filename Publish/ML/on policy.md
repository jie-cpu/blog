# Summary 

**On-Policy** refers to learning methods where the **agent evaluates and improves the same policy that it uses to interact with and explore the environment.**

- **Learning Policy = Behavior Policy:** The policy being learned (the "target" policy) is identical to the policy used to generate the data (the "behavior" policy).
- **Direct Improvement:** The agent directly improves the policy it's currently following.
