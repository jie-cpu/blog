# Summary 

MuJoCo (Multi-Joint dynamics with Contact) is a physics engine for simulating complex robotic systems.

### Key Points:

1. **Physics Simulation**: Accurately simulates movement and contact.
2. **Robotics and Biomechanics**: Models robots and human movements.
3. **High Performance**: Optimized for fast, real-time simulations.
