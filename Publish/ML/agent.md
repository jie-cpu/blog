# Summary 
 In reinforcement learning, an **agent** is a program or entity that makes decisions and takes actions in an environment to reach a goal.
1. **Decision-Maker**: The agent looks at the current situation and decides what to do next.
2. **Learning**: It learns from the rewards or penalties it gets after taking actions.
3. **Interaction**: The agent interacts with the environment by acting, receiving feedback, and improving its decisions.
